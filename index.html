About

Research that ships, and software that survives reality.

I'm an AI/ML researcher and engineer finishing an M.Sc. in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) at Leiden University, after a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran. My work sits where research meets shipping. I train models on one screen and deploy the product that uses them on the other.

Most of my time goes to two companies I co-founded. StudyPuff is a real-time study platform now used by 17,000+ students across 46 countries. AutoLabKimia is quality-control software for chemistry labs running ICP-OES analysis, in production at 30+ laboratories. Alongside that I work on deep learning and reinforcement learning, with published work on software defect prediction and counterfeit-tea detection.

I'm drawn to systems that survive contact with reality, like software that stays online through an internet shutdown, or models that still work on data they were never trained on. I'm open to research and engineering opportunities. The easiest way to reach me is email.
