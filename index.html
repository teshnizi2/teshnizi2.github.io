About

I'm an AI and machine-learning engineer and researcher. I'm finishing a master's in Computer Science (AI) at Leiden University, after a bachelor's in Computer Engineering at Sharif University in Tehran.

Most of my time goes to two startups I co-founded. StudyPuff is a study app for students, and AutoLabKimia runs quality control for chemistry labs. Alongside that, I research deep learning and reinforcement learning, with two published papers.
